2. Definitions

  • “Personal Data” means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
  • “Merchant” means a Shopify store owner who installs the App.
  • “Shop Data” means data belonging to the Merchant’s Shopify store (e.g., orders, products, cart contents) that is accessed through Shopify APIs.

3. Personal Data We Collect

Data we obtain from Shopify

  • Store Information – store name, myshopify.com domain, contact email, owner name.
  • Order Data – order IDs, item SKUs, quantities, prices and revenue totals.
  • Cart Data – product IDs, variants, quantities, prices.

Data we generate or log

  • Usage Metrics – widget impressions, clicks, upsell conversions, revenue generated by the App.
  • Technical Logs – IP address, user‑agent, timestamps, error traces.

Data you provide voluntarily

  • Support Communications – messages you send via Tawk.to live chat or email.
